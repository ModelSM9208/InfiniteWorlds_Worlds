So this is the first world I've made and I genuinely don't know what category it falls under other than mature. It's got some dark parts and some light parts. Premise is your a consciousness piloting a robot. Character select determines how initially human you want to be. I am putting it in NSFW because your robot may or may not get 'equipment' and two of the character options start with some.  What's suppose to be a big part of this framework is you navigating how people react to you robot shell and that can include physical intimacy. You can also keep all relationships completely platonic.
The external conflict is a doomsday cult that wants to destroy the world. A military that only sees victory through violence. And your gang of friends trying to stop that. The robot's personality is a blank slate at the start so if you want Dommy mommy hacker pick the witch and play it Dominant PC tag style. If you want to be a brick that doesn't know where babies come from you got the mauler. Gender is made up and anatomy has crafting components so it's in NSFW.
