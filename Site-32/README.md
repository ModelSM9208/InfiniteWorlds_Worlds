# InfiniteWorlds_Site-32

link to play: https://infiniteworlds.app/shared/pDkkPQ

Mature
content warnings: body horror, infection/disease, violence, gore, psychological horror, themes of mortality and human modification, isolation

Beneath the Nevada desert, in a decommissioned Cold War bunker redesignated R&D Site 32, something has gone terribly wrong. Elara Biologics — a biotech startup hemorrhaging money and burning through investor patience — was supposed to be building the future of medicine. Their crown jewel: VERIN, the Viral Engineered Regenerative Integration Network, a pathogen engineered to rewrite human biology from the inside out. A cure for death itself.

Now the containment seals are broken. The emergency lights paint everything red. The automated security system has classified every living thing in the facility as a biohazard. Your colleagues — the ones who aren't dead — aren't really your colleagues anymore.

And you can feel the VERIN already working inside you. A warmth behind your eyes. A tingling in your fingertips that wasn't there an hour ago.

Three sublevels stand between you and the surface. You'll need to activate the purge protocol in the deepest level of the facility to contain the outbreak, then claw your way topside through blast doors, automated turrets, and things that used to be people. Along the way, you'll find mutagen injectors — VERIN concentrates that can grant you inhuman abilities. Thermal vision. Bone-dense limbs. Accelerated healing. Each injection brings you closer to the exit. Each injection takes you further from what you were.

How much of yourself are you willing to lose to survive?
