# InfiniteWorlds_D.R.A.K.K.E.N.

Game link: https://infiniteworlds.app/shared/AywCon

Tokyo, 2198. You came to Akihabara for an anime convention. You woke up in an alley with your organs harvested and a classified military prototype fused to your chest cavity — one that's slowly turning you into a chrome-scaled dragon. You have 90 days before the trial license expires and the system keeping you alive shuts down. A megacorp CEO wants to own you. A hacktivist in a coffee shop wants to save you. The clock is ticking, and the only heart you have left keeps asking for a license key you don't have.
